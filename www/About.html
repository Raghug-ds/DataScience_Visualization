Overview

One of the main priorities for Mayor James Mueller is to reform public safety systems to make South Bendâ€™s residents safer. Our project goal was to design an interactive dashboard which can be a quick reference for South Bend leadership to gather a variety of information which can help with decision making on developing required amenities across the South Bend.

Neighborhood Development Areas

As part of this visualization, we will provide geographical boundaries of South Bend districts embedded in a map.

  • Sidebar panel of the plot will provide multiple options including Public Facilities, Schools , Parks , Abandon Areas which can be toggled using radio buttons.
  • Selecting a specific amenity will highlight the district boundary on map and the amenities associated within that district.
  • Clicking on specific district will provide the district and population details associated with in that district.
  • With or without District grouping will provide the flexibility of viewing the amenities without districts, where clicking on circle location will provide additional details about the facility.

Crime Prevention: Street Lighting

Research by the University of Chicago and Crime Lab New York has found that areas with increased lighting "experienced crime rates that were significantly lower" than those without.

  • A map visualization overlaying the location of light posts throughout the city with a different color depending on their brightness, will help understand the major gaps in lighting.
  • Viewing visualization without districts will provide the details on streetlight lumens, where pale light yellow provides insights to improve the streetlights.

Business Status and Location of Vacant Lots

There are numerous vacant lots across South Bend that have the potential to be developed into commercial property.

  • Visualization that shows the location of the vacant lots in South Bend and the locations of business licenses across the city.
  • This will aid in identifying areas for potential redevelopment with the goal of providing the city with more sources of revenue, especially in neighborhoods that would benefit from economic development.
  • Map provided will show vacant lots and location of business licenses per zip code to make it easier to identify neighborhoods that would benefit the most from revitalization.

Services and Call Data

Visually analyzing the call data log by the residents of the City of South Bend would identify the areas of concerns, needs, and allocate/prioritize the resources to address them.

  • Visualization with top 5 departments in terms of number of calls received and then the reasons on why the residents called.
  • Interactive visualization with a date slider will provide intuitive details on services requested over a period time.
  • A reactive department dropdown will provide the visualizations on service requests along with a time series plot.
